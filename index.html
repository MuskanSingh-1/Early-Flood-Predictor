How to Predict?

Follow these steps to quickly get district-wise flood risk alerts.

  1. Login or Sign Up using your account credentials.
  2. Click on the Predict button in the top menu.
  3. Choose your State from the dropdown list.
  4. Select the District you want flood risk for.
  5. Click on Predict and wait a moment.
  6. You will get:
    • Flood Risk Level (Low / Moderate / High)
    • Risk Score
    • Temperature
    • Humidity Level
    • Wind Speed
    • Rainfall Data
  7. Check Emergency Contacts if risk is Moderate or High.

Safety Tips

  • Verify weather updates regularly during monsoon.
  • Prepare an emergency kit for high-risk areas.
  • Stay away from rivers and low-lying zones during alerts.
  • Save helpline contacts for quick action.

Stay informed. Stay prepared. Stay safe.
